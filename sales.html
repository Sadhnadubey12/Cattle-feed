Premium Cattle Feed Recipes

With over 8 years of expertise in animal nutrition, YAS AGRO INDUSTRIES is committed to delivering premium cattle feed solutions that enhance the health and productivity of your livestock. Our recipes are formulated with precision to meet the nutritional requirements of animals, ensuring optimal performance and well-being.

This recipe features our high-quality cattle feed mix, enriched with essential nutrients for energy, growth, and immunity. Customize it by adding supplements like molasses for energy or grains for additional flavor. You can also include minerals for stronger immunity and better productivity.

In a clean container, mix our premium cattle feed blend with water to achieve the desired consistency. Adjust the quantity based on the age and nutritional needs of your livestock. Serve fresh to maximize nutrient absorption, and consider adding protein supplements for increased milk yield or weight gain.

Prepare fresh batches daily and provide clean water alongside. Store the feed in a cool, dry place to maintain its quality. Trust YAS AGRO INDUSTRIES to support your livestock's health with our years of experience and dedication to excellence.
