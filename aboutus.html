YAS AGRO INDUSTRIES - Premium Cattle Feed & Animal Nutrition

Yas Agro Industries is a professionally managed business that has been working in the field of cattle feed since 2015. The company has state-of-the-art facilities at Khandobachiwdi Tal.Mohol Dist.Solapur with a manufacturing capacity of 3000 metric tonnes of feed per Month under the brand name Hirkani Pashukhadya.

We at Yas Agro Industries understand that your cattle have specific nutrition needs at different stages of life. This is why we focus on sourcing high-quality ingredients to produce premium feed so that your animals give maximum performance.

Our operations are guided by the principle: "True success lies in the progress of our customers, suppliers, business partners, and the community." Supported by core values of integrity, transparency, and respect, we offer a wide range of animal nutrition solutions.
