Premium Cattle Feed & Animal Nutrition

YAS AGRO INDUSTRIES is dedicated to providing high-quality cattle feed and animal nutrition products. Our premium products include Bal Shakti, Garbh Shakti, Milk Shakti, and Pashu Khad (3, 4, and 5 Star). These products are formulated to meet the nutritional needs of livestock, ensuring healthy growth, better productivity, and overall well-being. We focus on delivering the best nutrition for your animals to help them thrive.
